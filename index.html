Nenet

Born Maria Florencia Barrios in Buenos Aires, June 2, 1986, Nenet is an argentine writer, singer and performer. Her first steps in poetry were tearjerking odes to denim jackets, contemptuous to authority and highschool. She edits fanzines of her own poetry, pop culture and surrealist musings. In 2005 she starts "Vida Yummy", an online archive that narrates the misfortunes of work, love and reviews Buenos Aires underground musical scene. After a brief period studying Literature in the University of Buenos Aires (UBA), she edits an EP together with the band Amenaza Menor. With the writer and visual artist Liv Schulman she publishes the magazine "Pobre, Feo & Elegante". In 2012, after moving to the US, she publishes the bilingual feminist fanzine "Leda", followed by "Medea". In 2014 she teaches "Poetry & Sodomy: Argentine Writers of the 20th Century" for Knowledge Commons DC. After a ghostly career in singing songwriting, she edits "Demo", which she presents in San Telmo (BA) along with the band Mujercitas Terror. She currently resides in Washington, DC.

Nacida Maria Florencia Barrios en Buenos Aires, en Junio 2 de 1986, Nenet es una escritora, performer y cantante argentina. De la mano de su profesor de Lengua y Literatura, el poeta Carlos Battilana, comenzo a escribir poesia y odas lamentables a las campera de jean y el desprecio a la autoridad y la escuela secundaria. Edita fanzines de cultura pop y surrealismo en castellano e ingles. En 2005 comienza Vida Yummy, su proyecto permanente en el que narra las desventuras de las primeras experiencias laborales, afectivas y cronicas sesgadas de la escena del under porteno. Ingresa y abandona la Facultad de Filosofia y Letras. Organiza lecturas y recitales, edita un EP junto a la banda Amenaza Menor. Funda junto a Liv Schulman la revista "Pobre, Feo y Elegante". Publica en 2013 el fanzine feminista "Leda", seguido por "Medea" en 2015. Luego de una espectral carrera en la musica, edita "Demo", el cual presenta en San Telmo junto a la banda Mujercitas Terror. Reside actualmente en la ciudad de Washington, DC.
